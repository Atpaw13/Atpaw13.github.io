Who We Are

MoreThanMyGPA is a HOSA mental health initiative dedicated to helping students recognize their inherent worth beyond academic performance.

We focus on cultivating resilience through positive psychology, community support, and evidence-based mental health practices. Our mission is to create a culture where students feel valued for their whole selves, not just their grades.

Through social media engagement and campus initiatives, we provide practical mindfulness techniques, stress management strategies, and reminders that success comes in many forms. We believe that by nurturing mental wellness, students can achieve both personal fulfillment and academic success.

Join us in building a community where every student knows they are more than a letter grade, and where mental health is prioritized as the foundation for all achievement.
